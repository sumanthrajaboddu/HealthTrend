# Success Criteria

## User Success

- Uncle logs at least 2–3 of 4 daily time slots consistently
- Single entry takes under 5 seconds from app open to done
- No data gaps longer than 2–3 days in the Google Sheet
- PDF report is used in at least one doctor visit and provides actionable insight
- Uncle completes first entry within 60 seconds of first launch with zero confusion

## Business Success

- N/A — personal-use app with no commercial objectives
- Success metric: does Uncle's doctor make better treatment decisions because of the data?

## Technical Success

- Zero app crashes or sync failures per week
- Data appears correctly in Google Sheet after each save
- Full offline functionality — identical experience online and offline
- Reminders fire reliably at configured times
- Smooth performance on mid-range Android devices

## Measurable Outcomes

| KPI | Target | How Measured |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| Entries per week | 20+ (of 28 possible) | Google Sheet row count |
| App crashes / sync failures | 0 per week | Manual observation |
| PDF generated before doctor visit | At least 1 per visit | User feedback |
| Time from app open to entry saved | < 5 seconds | UX observation |
| First entry completion (new user) | < 60 seconds | Setup observation |
| Analytics show meaningful trends | After 1 week of data | Visual inspection |
